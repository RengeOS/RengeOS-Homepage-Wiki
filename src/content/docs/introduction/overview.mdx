## What's this?
**RengeOS** is an Arch Linux-based distribution focused on minimalism, performance, stability, and high self-maintenance,
 allowing you to repair your system on faulty partitions using Recovery Mode.

| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Processor** | 2 GHz Dual Core | 2 GHz Quad Core |
| **RAM** | 2 GB | 4 GB |
| **Storage** | 21 GB | 45 GB |
